Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our investment platform and services.

How do I start investing with Finvest?

To start investing, simply register an account, verify your email, make a deposit using any of our supported payment methods (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Upay, or Bank Transfer), and select an investment plan. Your daily profits will start accruing immediately.

When will I receive my daily profits?

Daily profits are automatically calculated and credited to your wallet every 24 hours after your investment becomes active. The exact time depends on when you made your deposit, but typically profits are added at midnight (Bangladesh time).

How long does it take to process withdrawals?

Withdrawal requests are typically processed within 2-6 hours during business hours. For bKash, Nagad, and Rocket, withdrawals are usually instant once approved. Bank transfers may take 1-3 business days depending on your bank.

Is there any referral program?

Yes! We offer a generous referral program where you can earn 5% of your referral's first deposit and 2% of their subsequent deposits. You can find your unique referral link in your dashboard to share with friends and family.

Is my investment secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level SSL encryption to protect all data and transactions. Our investment strategies are managed by professional financial experts with years of experience. Additionally, we maintain complete transparency about our operations and returns.
